Special Events

Kids Day, Saturday, Oct. 12

Our haunted outdoor trail is not just for adults. Even your younger spooks will find something to entertain them. We offer events that your younger family members will enjoy. In addition to trick or treating in the first few rooms of our trail (our people will not be dressed up as they are at night,) we will also have a costume contest, arts & crafts, a bounce house/ slide, food, fun, games, prizes! Come join us October 12th from 9 am until noon. Tickets for this event are only purchasable the day of the event and can not be purchased online. Cash, Credit cards, and Apple Pay accepted.


Special Needs, Special Event
Wednesday, Oct. 16, 7:30 - 10:00

A Petrified Forest welcomes everyone with special needs to attend and go through the trails! We design this night to be experienced at your own pace. If you want to stop and take a break, no problem. If you need assistance with your wheelchair, we've got just the guys to be there. This night is only open to those with special needs and their group. Tickets for this event are only purchasable the day of the event and can not be purchased online.


Total Eclipse Nights
Wedenesday, Oct. 30

You... Our monsters... 1 Glow Stick... No lights!

Each person will be given a glow stick to light their path if they so choose. If your glowstick is lost or stolen by a monster or creature, it will not be returned. Combo Packages available

What will you encounter? Will you get out alive or will the monsters get you? Will yo be separated from your group and be lost forever?

Note that this event is not for the faint of heart or the easily frightened. As with all of our events, this event is rated PG-13. Children 12 and under must be accompanied by an adult.

Anyone under 18 participating in Total Eclipse Nights must have a parent's signature. Download the Waiver Waiver may be filled out and brought to A Petrified Forest on the night you attend.


Backstage Tours, Every Night

Ever wondered what it takes to operate a Haunted House attraction?

Do you want to experience the scare trail without being in the middle of the action? Have you ever wanted to be a scare actor in a haunted attraction? A Petrified Forest is proud to be the only Halloween haunted attraction in Central Florida that offers backstage tours. This is also a great opportunity for your younger or less brave family members to experience the excitement of our haunted attraction without having to go through the trail. While on this guided your, you will see a behind-the scenes look at many of our sets, meet some of the cast and crew, learn a few tricks of the trade, and be part of the show by scaring some of the guests.

How good of a scare actor can you be? Find out by being a part of one of our nightly backstage tours. Combo Packages available
